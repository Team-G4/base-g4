Welcome to G4.7

Welcome to G4.7!

G4 is a game inspired by The Theorist Gateway's Gate 4 (that's why it's called "G4", fun fact).
It started off as an "infinite version" of Gate 4, but now it could be considered a completely separate, full game.

Objective

Shoot through the rotating rings of obstacles. When the bullet escapes the construct, you get to the next level.
You can use your finger, mouse, keyboard or gamepad to play the game.

G4 Account

Create a G4 account to get your scores on the leaderboard and collect achievements.
